Our Story

Born from
Smoke & Passion

Akbare Sekuwa & Restro was forged in the heart of Chitwan — a land of wilderness and warmth. What began as a family tradition of grilling over open fire evolved into a philosophy: food cooked with real flame holds a soul that no oven can replicate.

We source only the finest local cuts, marinate them in ancestral spice blends, and char-grill them over hardwood charcoal. The result is not merely a meal — it is an experience rooted in Nepali heritage, elevated to a premium dining art form.
